About Me

Hi! I'm ANABELLA ROCCO

3D Modeling, Environment Design, 2D Art and Concept Artistry.

I consider myself a high-level artist, creative, enthusiastic and professional with a mastery in the latest technologies and design tools.


I'm specialized in environment and prop modeling/texturing/surfacing.


I can manage the complete process from 3D blocking to 3D sculpt, 3D modeling, importing in Game Engines, UV layout, texture shading, baking, lighting and final rendering.
