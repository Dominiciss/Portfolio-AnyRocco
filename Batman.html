Batmobile

Personal Project

The iconic Batmobile has been modelled in detail using Blender 3D modelling software. Precise shapes and characteristic details have been created, capturing the essence of this iconic vehicle. Once the modelling was complete, Pixar's RenderMan rendering engine was used to generate the final image.
